‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to know finance?

No! We teach from fundamentals. Strong quantitative skills are what matter‚Äîwe'll teach you the finance.

Do I need to code?

Basic Python is helpful but you can learn along the way. We'll provide resources and support for developing programming skills.

What's the time commitment?

4-6 hours weekly for most active members. This includes meetups, problem sets, and review time. Comparable to a challenging course.

Is there a membership fee?

Free membership! Club is funded through DTU student activities and will seek external sponsorship as we grow.

Can I join mid-semester?

We prefer semester start for cohesive learning, but contact us‚Äîwe may accommodate exceptional cases.

What if I'm not sure quant finance is for me?

Attend our intro sessions to learn more before committing! We'll explain the career path, job types, and what to expect.

What year should I join?

Any year works! First-years benefit from early start, but third-years and Master's students actively recruiting also join for intensive preparation.

Do you help with internships and full-time positions?

Yes! Mock interviews, resume reviews, firm introductions, and recruiting strategy guidance. As our network grows, we'll provide direct connections to trading firms.
