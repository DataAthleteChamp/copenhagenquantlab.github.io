About Copenhagen Quant Lab

Our Story

Copenhagen Quant Lab was founded in 2025 by DTU students who recognized a critical gap: DTU produces world-class engineers and mathematicians, yet few enter quantitative finance despite having ideal skills.


The industry remains opaque, recruiting processes are mysterious, and most students don't know where to start. Only 7 DTU graduates have historically entered quantitative finance—a number we aim to change dramatically.


Our mission: By building the right environment, every dedicated member will have a real shot at getting into a top trading firm.

What We Believe

  • Excellence compounds — We prioritize depth over breadth, mastering foundational concepts before advancing.
  • Community accelerates growth — We learn faster together through peer teaching and collaborative problem-solving.
  • Deliberate practice matters — We emphasize structured repetition of interview-style problems rather than passive learning.
  • Small actions accumulate — Consistent weekly engagement matters more than intensity sprints.
  • Diversity of thought strengthens analysis — We welcome all DTU students regardless of background, seeking intellectual curiosity and work ethic.
  • Transparency builds trust — We're honest about our starting point, ambitious goals, and progress along the way.

Practical Information

Meeting Schedule: Weekly meetups every Wednesday 18:00-20:00, Building 101 Room 3C

Time Commitment: 4-6 hours weekly (2h meetup + 2-3h problem sets + 1h review/reading)

Requirements: Strong quantitative skills, genuine interest in trading, commitment to consistent participation

Cost: Free membership (club funded through DTU student activities)
