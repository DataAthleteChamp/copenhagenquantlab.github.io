Learning Resources

Curated materials for mastering quantitative finance, from probability foundations through advanced trading strategies.

Probability & Statistics

Foundational knowledge for all quantitative work.

Fifty Challenging Problems in Probability with Solutions Classic collection of elegant probability puzzles. Perfect for building intuition and problem-solving skills. Start here if you're comfortable with basic probability.
Introduction to Probability Comprehensive textbook covering probability theory from fundamentals through advanced topics. Harvard's Stat 110 course uses this textâ€”videos available free online.
Brilliant.org Probability Course Interactive online learning with immediate feedback. Covers expected value, distributions, conditional probability, and more.

Options & Derivatives

Understanding financial instruments and pricing theory.

Options, Futures, and Other Derivatives by John Hull Industry standard textbook. Focus on chapters 1-15 and 20-23. Dense but comprehensive.
Paul Wilmott Introduces Quantitative Finance More intuitive than Hull, with practical examples and trading perspectives.
OptionsPlaybook.com Interactive tool for visualizing options strategies. Essential for building intuition about payoff profiles.

Algorithmic Trading

Systematic strategy development and implementation.

Algorithmic Trading by Ernest Chan Practical guide to developing trading strategies. Covers mean reversion, momentum, statistical arbitrage with Python examples.
QuantConnect Platform Cloud-based backtesting platform with free tier. Code strategies in Python, backtest on historical data.

Interview Preparation

Resources for quantitative finance recruiting.

Heard on the Street by Timothy Crack Essential interview preparation covering probability, statistics, logic, and finance brain teasers.
A Practical Guide to Quantitative Finance Interviews 150+ interview questions with detailed solutions. Updated regularly with new questions.
BrainStellar.com Free website with probability and logic puzzles similar to trading interview questions.
