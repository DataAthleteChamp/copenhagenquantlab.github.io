Our Mission: By building the right environment, every dedicated member will have a real shot at getting into a top trading firm.

Copenhagen Quant Lab provides the structured environment, rigorous training, and peer community that transforms motivated DTU students into competitive candidates for quantitative trading and research roles. In an industry where only 7 DTU graduates have historically succeeded, we're building a deliberate pipeline through weekly problem sets, mock interviews, trading competitions, and strategic use of poker and chess for developing probabilistic thinking.
