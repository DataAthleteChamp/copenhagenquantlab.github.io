Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join?

Visit our Join page for detailed information about the joining process.

When do you meet?

Weekly meetups every Wednesday 18:00-20:00 at DTU. Additional poker nights on Fridays and monthly events.

Can I visit before joining?

Absolutely! Email us to attend an intro session or visit a meetup as a guest.

Do you have industry connections?

We're actively building partnerships with Nordic and international trading firms. As we grow, members will get priority access to recruiting events and firm connections.

I'm a company interested in partnering. Who do I contact?

Email partners@copenhagenquantlab.com to discuss sponsorship, guest speaking, recruiting partnerships, or other collaboration opportunities.
